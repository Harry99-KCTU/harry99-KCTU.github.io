Scratch - Brick Breaker

Instructions: Left button to move left, and vice-versa.

Objective: : The objective of brick breaker is to hit all 28 of the bricks without the ball missing the paddle and hitting the ground in a single life. 

Notes: Score can be seen on the top-left hand side of the screen, and will update manually every time a block is cleared.
